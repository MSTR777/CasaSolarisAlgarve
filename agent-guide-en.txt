WHERE IT IS
Burgau is a small, whitewashed fishing village often nicknamed "a Santorini portuguesa" for its cascading white, blue-trimmed houses above a working harbour. It sits between Praia da Luz and Lagos on the Western Algarve, inside the protected Costa Vicentina coastline, which has kept it free of large-scale resort development.
